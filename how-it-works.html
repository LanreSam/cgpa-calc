How this application works

This application is a stand alone application and is in no way tied to any University's websites as at this period. Therefore, you are adviced to go to your dashboard on the school's portal and write out the following on a piece of paper Course Code, Course Units, Score, Semester & Level of each course you did in each semester of the academic year you wish to add to your dashboard on this application. This is a one time activity, as your inputs will always be stored in this application and readily available for future reference as you don't have to go through all the stress anymore.
I'm so sorry for the stress. I don't have access to your records on your School's database.

How OOU calculates GPA

Every course in OOU has a Course unit (CU). Therefore to get the Quality Point (QP) for a particular course in any semester, OOU multiplies the Course Unit (CU) by the Grade Point (GP) earned by the student in that particular course.
For Example: Lanre signed up for 20 Course units in Rain Semester, and got a Quality Point of 85 for that Semester. Therefore, OOU calculates Lanre's Grade Point Average GPA for that particular semester using the formula GPA = QP/CU GPA = 85/20 GPA = 4.25 Therefore Lanre's GPA for Rain Semester is 4.25

How OOU calculates CGPA

Here is a simple explanation of how OOU calculates CGPA. For Example: Lanre had a QP of 75 in Hamattan Semester and a QP of 85 in Rain Semester of the same academic year and he did a Total Course Unit (TCU) of 37 for that academic year. Then we can use the formula CGPA = TQP/TCU to calculate his CGPA for that academic year.
CGPA = (75 + 85)/37 CGPA = 160/37
CGPA = 4.32
Therefore Lanre's CGPA for that academic year is 4.32 OOU repeats the process until you graduate from the university.
